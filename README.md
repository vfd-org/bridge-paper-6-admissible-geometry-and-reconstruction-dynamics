## ðŸ” Scope and Intent

This paper:

- Treats stabilised geometries as elements of a configuration space \( \mathcal{G} \)
- Defines **admissible histories** via stability and bounded mismatch constraints
- Formulates reconstruction as a **selection rule** on \( \mathcal{G} \)
- Shows how inertial/geodesic structure emerges in the deep-stabilisation/slow regime
- Introduces interaction in the IR as **constraint coupling** on \( \mathcal{G} \times \mathcal{G} \)

This paper **does not** claim:
- a derivation of Lorentz symmetry or GR from first principles
- a quantum theory or quantisation procedure
- a fundamental ontology of spacetime
- observer-primacy or consciousness-based dynamics
